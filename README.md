🚀 Chocolate Retail Chain Performance & Insights Dashboard 🍫
🌍 Overview

An interactive Business Intelligence project built using Power BI and Python to analyze the performance of a chocolate retail company operating across 100 stores.

This project transforms raw sales data into actionable business insights through data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), data modeling, and interactive dashboard design.

🔍 Business Problem

In 2024, Total Quantity Sold increased by more than 200% 📈

Logically, Total Revenue should have increased significantly.
However, the surprising result was:

❌ Total Revenue increased by only 0.2%

This revealed major performance issues that required deeper analysis to identify the root causes behind weak revenue growth despite the high sales volume.

📊 Key Insights

✅ Dark Chocolate 50% was the top-performing product:

Achieved +7.5% profit
Exceeded target by 5%

❌ Truffle Chocolate 60% underperformed:

Recorded -1.4% loss
Failed to achieve target

📉 Customer count started declining, indicating ineffective discount strategies and poor product optimization.

🌍 Canada generated the highest overall revenue.

✈️ Airport stores achieved the best sales performance.

👥 Customers aged 50+ represented the most active customer segment.

📅 The fourth quarter (Q4) was the strongest sales period of the year.
